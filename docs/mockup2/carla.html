Carla

Lives Homeless in LA


Hi I'm Carla, and my kids Paula and Steven are 10 and 14.

We've been living in the Valley for 12y, since Steven was 3. My kids love their school.


We had a good apartment in Van Nuys until the building was sold and the rent was raised. I couldn't afford it.


We're lucky and have a small room at a shelter for now. Before that we just slept in the gym hall with a lot of other people... All the money I make goes to what my kids need and I'm applying for affordable housing.

After I drop my kids at school, I go to my part time job as a janitor. I'm looking for a new FT job, but it's been hard to apply. I have to take care of my kids.
