Ron

Lives Homeless in LA


Hello, my name is Ron, I'm 82 years old. I was married for 60 years but my wife Sheila passed away 6 months ago. I have three kids, five grandkids and two great grandkids, but I don't get to see them much at the moment...

I was born in Venice and lived in LA for almost all of my life, except for the time I served in Vietnam.


Six months ago my wife Sheila passed away. She was sick for a long time before that and I wasn't doing too well. Soon after that I couldn't keep paying my rent and started living in my car.


Right now I live in my car, most nights I stay at the parking lot by the boardwalk. It's getting colder at night and sometimes it doesn't feel very safe, but thereare always people around, which is nice...

During the day, I generally stay around Venice, there's a nice community here. I go to People Concern every few days to get a shower and some food, I have a little money from my pension. It's not a lot but it helps...
