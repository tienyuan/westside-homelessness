Melissa & Pepper

Lives Homeless in LA


Hi, I'm Pepper, I'm 7 years old and very cute. I live with my roommate, Melissa!

Melissa has been in Los Angeles for 20 years and I've been here since I was born in Hollywood!


We use to live with Melissa's husband until he started hitting us. Melissa said we couldn't stay.


Now, we live in our car and SafeParkingLA gives us a safe place to park each night. We leave every morning to go to work and we're saving for a new home!

Melissa works very hard! She is a professional dog trainer and teaches other dogs how to be good boys! I am the best boy of course.
