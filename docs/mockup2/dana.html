Dana

Lives Homeless in LA


Hey, I'm Dana and I'm 21.

I've lived in LA since I was 12 years old, I came here with my mum from New Orleans but she couldn't take care of me, so I ended up in foster care here.


Once I turned 18 I couldn't stay in foster care anymore. I stayed with one of my friend's family for a bit but eventually they asked me to leave because they needed to rent their spare room out and I couldn't pay.


I'm not really homeless, I stay with friends often, sometimes I go to this shelter for youth, and every now and then I end up sleeping outside...

I'm taking classes at Santa Monica community college and trying to find a real gig. I'm delivering stuff in between classes on my bike for now. Gotta hustle!
